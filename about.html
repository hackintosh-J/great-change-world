About This Project

This website is an evolving playground of interactive demonstrations and simulations. Our goal is to inspire curiosity about mathematics, science and technology through engaging visual experiments.

We believe that simple rules can lead to beautiful complexity. From fractals to cellular automata and epidemic models, each page shows how code can illuminate patterns in nature and society.

本项目方向透过交互式演示和模拟情感发人们对数学、科学和技术的好奇心。我们相信，简单的规则可以创造出令人惊艳的复杂性。欢迎探索这个不断成长的世界！

Feel free to explore, learn and modify the code. Every experiment is written in plain JavaScript and runs entirely in your browser.
